Located at s.nerd.nu, the Survival server offers a more competitive atmosphere for Minecraft multiplayer. With map wide PVP, surviving is a challenge. Individual players and organized clans rise and fall through the revisions; with glory and fame to be had by those who take it.

If you are tired of how easily you can beat back the zombies and skeletons then this is a server for you. While traveling, building or mining, nowhere is safe. Minecraft is a challenge again with the constant threat of death and the wonderful allure of others' treasures. Your builds are that much more impressive, your victory in battle and the loot that you gain more satisfying in this environment. It is for those who enjoy a challenge.
